Channel Mix

Traditional Channels (GT/MT)

  • Vast majority of durables still sold through fragmented multi-brand retail + regional distributors
  • Extreme footfall volatility. B2B digital sales platform shift underway to improve demand forecasting
  • Aggressive pre-BEE channel stuffing compressed Q3 gross margins via heavy promotional discounting

D2C & Quick Commerce

  • D2C CAC crisis: Customer acquisition costs skyrocketed. Organic discovery vanished, replaced by expensive paid performance marketing
  • Quick commerce: High visibility for small electricals but fails to generate brand loyalty. Reduces products to pure commodities
  • Brands executing painful pivots: localizing packaging, SKU rationalization, redesigning fulfillment to lower RTO rates

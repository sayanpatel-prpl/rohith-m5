## 2. Operational Intelligence from Management Commentary

**Insight:** Post-Crompton acquisition (March 2022), company executed a fundamental structural reset — transitioning from promoter-driven to merit-driven culture, implementing SNOP-driven planning, real-time distributor tracking, and channel rebalancing. FY24 was a deliberately managed decline with pricing/trade-spend restructuring. FY25 H2 and FY26 show successful margin recovery.

**Key Signals:**
- Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Limited acquired majority stake March 2022
- Scheme of Amalgamation with Crompton approved March 2023
- Plant consolidation: "consolidated the operations of one plant into the main plant"
- Automated production line for pressure cookers + laser-cutting machine added (FY25)
- SNOP (Sales and Operations Planning) as backbone for demand-supply alignment
- Real-time inventory tracking at distributor and dealer level
- Project "Munnetram": cross-functional cost optimization programme — Rs.1.5 Cr savings
- New brand identity: "Celebrating Change" (Q1 FY26); 40+ SKU 'Idea First Series' launched
- New Head of Manufacturing hired (Somasundaram P, from EV/automotive, w.e.f. Jan 2026)
- New Head of Downstream Marketing hired (Muthu Saravanan K, ex-EID Parry, Philips, CavinKare, w.e.f. Dec 2025)

**Evidence:** "Strategic interventions aimed at driving business revival — revised pricing structure, improved management of trade spends, shift in marketing strategies, focus on enhancing commercial hygiene." — *AR FY25 MD&A*. "5.41% growth with 7.83% EBITDA" in H2 FY25 — *AR FY25 Chairman's Message*

**Source:** INTEGRATED_GOVERNANCE (AR FY23, AR FY25) · QUARTERLY_FINANCIAL_REPORT (Q2 FY26)

**Confidence:** High

## 3. Capacity / Plant Utilization

**Insight:** Fully integrated manufacturing with ~80% in-house production. No explicit utilization % disclosed. Plant consolidation indicates rationalization of excess capacity. Capex declined sharply (FY24 Rs.2,125 Lakhs → FY25 Rs.853 Lakhs), signalling no major greenfield expansion.

**Evidence:**
- "With almost 80% of the products manufactured in-house, the Company is well insulated from third-party dependent supply chain disruptions." — *AR FY21 MD&A*
- Products manufactured in-house: LPG Stoves, Mixer Grinders, Pressure Cookers, Table-Top Wet Grinders, Vacuum Flasks — *Q3 FY22 Investor Presentation*
- "Consolidated the operations of one plant into the main plant, which has helped to improve efficiency and reduce costs." — *AR FY25 MD&A*
- "Established an automated production line for pressure cookers and introduction of a laser-cutting machine for precision fabrication." — *AR FY25 MD&A*
- Non-renewable energy capacity: 24,245 GJ (stable FY24 = FY25) — *AR FY25 KPIs*
- CapEx: FY25 Rs.853 Lakhs vs FY24 Rs.2,125 Lakhs vs FY21 Rs.1,009 Lakhs — *AR FY21, AR FY25*
- 1 state-of-the-art R&D facility with in-house design studio, dedicated team of 15 people — *Q3 FY22 Investor Presentation*

**Source:** INTEGRATED_GOVERNANCE (AR FY21, AR FY25) · INVESTOR_ANALYST (Q3 FY22)

**Confidence:** Medium *(utilization % not disclosed; directional signals available)*

## Strategic Signals Summary

### Key Opportunities

1. **Pan-India market expansion via Crompton synergies** — currently ~5% pan-India vs ~15% South; vast untapped territory with Crompton's distribution as lever
2. **Premiumization via 'Idea First Series'** — premium launches gaining traction on E-com and Modern Trade; ASP uplift visible in material margins (+320 bps Q1 FY26)
3. **GST reduction tailwinds** — "GST reduction coupled with festivities led to buoyancy in kitchen categories, with pressure cookers benefitting the most" (Q2 FY26)
4. **E-com + Quick Commerce growth** — premium offerings driving disproportionate growth in E-com, Modern Trade and Regional Chain Stores
5. **After-sales as competitive moat** — industry-leading turnaround time acknowledged by e-commerce partners; 280+ service centres and CRM infrastructure building loyalty
6. **Energy-efficient product portfolio** — India's first star-rated gas stove; aligned to green consumer trends and regulatory tailwinds
7. **Localization deepening** — room to further reduce import dependence; structural cost reduction with supply chain resilience

### Key Risks

1. **Revenue trajectory** — Revenue 18% below FY23 peak (Rs.865 Cr FY25 vs Rs.1,057 Cr FY23); recovery pace uncertain
2. **Steel single-sourcing** — largest direct material with no alternate vendor; significant cost concentration risk
3. **MFI channel suppression** — explicitly cited as headwind in FY25; rural demand vulnerability remains
4. **South India concentration** — ~75–76% revenue from South; pan-India expansion dependent on Crompton integration execution
5. **Online competition intensity** — strong competition from global and domestic brands requires sustained A&P investment
6. **Auxiliary category decline** — SKU rationalization improves EBITDA but reduces revenue buffer; risk if core categories slow
7. **Crompton merger integration risk** — Scheme of Amalgamation approved March 2023; integration complexity and governance transition ongoing

### Cost Strategy Direction

Deliberate **margin-accretive cost management** playbook underway:
- Localization savings (Rs.45 L FY25)
- SNOP-driven inventory normalization (slow-moving inventory –86%)
- A&P ROI discipline (+351 bps EBITDA expansion)
- Plant consolidation and automation (pressure cooker line)
- Finance cost near-zero (Rs.519 Lakhs FY25 vs Rs.23 Cr FY17)
- Project Munnetram cross-functional savings (Rs.1.5 Cr)

**Net result:** EBITDA margin expanding from 2.94% (FY24) → 7.55% (FY25) → 9.5% (Q2 FY26) — a **651 bps recovery in 6 quarters**

### Market Position Direction

**Consolidation + selective expansion:** Defend South India leadership (#1 LPG stoves and TTWGs; Top 3 mixer grinders). Target pan-India scale via Crompton synergies. Premiumize portfolio to capture higher ASPs. Reduce dependence on gas dealer and MFI channels in favour of retail, modern trade and online. New brand identity ("Celebrating Change") signals aspiration to evolve from South-Indian kitchen specialist to national premium kitchen and domestic appliances brand.

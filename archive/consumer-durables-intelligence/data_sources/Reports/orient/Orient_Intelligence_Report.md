## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Orient Electric Limited is executing a multi-product premiumization strategy across three core segments: **Electrical Consumer Durables (ECD)** (~60% revenue), **Lighting & Switchgear** (~30%), and emerging categories. The company is navigating regulatory transitions (BEE Star ratcheting for ceiling fans effective Jan 1, 2026) while building BLDC fan share from ~30% to a target of 45% in next 2 years. Strong double-digit growth in ECD heating (20% YoY), lighting (~18.6% YoY), and wires (100%+ YoY on small base) are balanced against softer domestic fan volumes. Exports are growing 40% YoY, driven by international expansion. The company is pursuing a hybrid MD/DTM (Direct-to-Market) model to capture market share in tier-2/3 cities.

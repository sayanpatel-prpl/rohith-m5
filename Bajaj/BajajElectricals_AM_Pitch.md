### Why Now

Q3 FY26 operating loss (-Rs 20.28 Cr PBT). Board pre-authorized minimum director remuneration — admission of anticipated full-year FY26 losses. New MD Sanjay Sachdeva (March 2025) inherited 43% capacity utilization, -15% retailer base decline, and Rs 134 Cr PBT swing in 9 months. Classic turnaround window: new CEO + operating crisis + liquidity buffer (Rs 509 Cr surplus).

### Top A&M Levers

1. **Manufacturing Footprint Rationalization** — CP capacity utilization collapsed to 43% — 10.8 Mn units installed but only 4.6 Mn produced. Four plants and 31.5 lakh sq.ft. warehousing built for volumes not materializing. Estimated impact: +60 bps (Rs 25 Cr).
2. **Operations & Cost Efficiency** — Other expenses surged +17.5% in Q3 FY26 (Rs 215 Cr vs Rs 183 Cr in Q3 FY25) despite -5% revenue decline — cost structure not flexing down. Estimated impact: +50 bps (Rs 21 Cr).
3. **Procurement & Sourcing** — Traded goods constitute ~73% of total material cost (Rs 552 Cr traded vs Rs 135 Cr raw). High outsourced dependency dilutes margin leverage when volumes fall. Estimated impact: +45 bps (Rs 19 Cr).

Bajaj Electricals Disconnect
The Narrative
“Improvement in gross margins” (MD letter FY25). “Built for Life” premiumisation drive with extended warranty on 23 SKUs. FY25 recovery narrative: trade channel +6%, air coolers +65%, dealer base +21% to 850+.
What the Numbers Say
The company is in operating loss territory — the recovery narrative has no financial foundation.
  • Q3 FY26 PBT: −Rs 20.28 Cr — operating loss, not recovery
  • 9MFY26 revenue −9.7% YoY to Rs 3,210 Cr — top-line shrinking
  • Board pre-approved minimum remuneration for anticipated FY26 full-year losses
  • CP utilization at 43% — over half capacity idle
  • Retailer base −15% (2L → 1.7L) — channel contracting, not expanding
  • Other expenses +17.5% in a loss quarter — no cost discipline
  • Rs 28.89 Cr exceptional restructuring charge — workforce downsizing underway

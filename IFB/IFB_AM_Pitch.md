### Executive Summary

IFB Industries is a Rs 5,127 Cr (FY25 consolidated) consumer durables company operating two divisions: a growing but margin-challenged Home Appliances Division (washers, ACs, refrigerators, MWOs, dishwashers, kitchen) and a high-margin Engineering Division (fine blanking/stamping for auto OEMs, Rs 835 Cr, 15.9% PBDIT, 34% ROCE). Despite delivering its best-ever revenue year in FY25 (+14.6%), EBITDA margins remain stuck at 5.63% --- well below the repeatedly guided double-digit target and 400+ bps behind named peers Crompton (9.8%) and Butterfly (10.0%). A&M is already engaged since February 2025 on a Rs 200 Cr material cost reduction mandate with Rs 35 Cr realized in 9 months, but the appliance business faces a 16+ month CEO vacancy, 7+ simultaneous strategic initiatives, and gross margin compression from 43% to 38-40%. The window to expand scope from material cost reduction into fixed cost right-sizing, portfolio rationalization, and interim CEO placement is open now, while credibility from the initial mandate is high.
